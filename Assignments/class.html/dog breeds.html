The Dalmatian is a breed of dog,which has a white coat marked with black or brown-coloured spots. Originating as a hunting dog,it was also used as a carriage dog in its early days. The origins of this breed can be traced back to present-day Croatia and its historical region of Dalmatia.It is thought that early ancestors of the breed were certain breeds of pointers and a spotted Great Dane.Today, it is a popular family pet and many dog enthusiasts enter Dalmatians into kennel club competitions.
